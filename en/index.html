In the villages of the Republic of Moldova, there is a lack of prospects, high unemployment and migration are the result, broken families and lonely senior citizens are a visible reality.

We believe in a hopeful future. These wonderful people need encouragement, inspiration, community and protection.

At VIVOS we live community and offer courses and trainings for teenagers, care staff and community founders.

actions speak louder than words

«That might work in the capital» is an answer we often get when we try to encourage people in the villages to try new ways.

That is why our VIVOS community is at home in a village outside the capital Chisinau. If we can strengthen the local community with simple means in this village, we can do the same in other villages.

At our day center, we model child-centered care at eye level. We take the children's challenges and problems seriously and help them become self-confident adults who say "No!" to alcohol abuse and violence and stand up for their personal integrity.

Educate foster

If we have been able to inspire people, we offer practical help in implementing the change.

Child care

We offer basic training for care personnel, which is loosely based on the dual Swiss education model. In order to use synergies and resources efficiently, we cooperate with other organizations.

Community

At VIVOS we test simple, low-cost ways to live community. We offer the findings in a way that is easy to understand and train those who are interested.

Teenager

We offer courses and activities for teenagers. In addition, we create a platform for the local population to pass on their own passion to the next generation and build up a small extra income.

Founder and Doer

We participate in the local startup and design community with workshops and meet-ups.

Our teens at the co-working space automatically come into contact with innovators and learn enterpreneurship. We also offer design thinking and IT courses for them.
